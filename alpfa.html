Association of Latino Professionals For America (ALPFA)

2024

Identity + Website Design
A compelling pro-bono opportunity to showcase visual design expertise by revitalizing ALPFA’s (Association of Latino Professionals For America) public-facing website. Our goals are clear: to elevate membership engagement and solidify our reputation as leaders in design excellence. Emphasizing a mobile-first strategy, we prioritize seamless user experiences with desktop optimization scheduled for mid-2024. In this redesign, we're committed to captivating the younger generation of Latino American Professionals. Our approach carefully balances professionalism and maturity with a welcoming, approachable tone, staying true to the brand's essence while resonating deeply with our intended audience.

+high-fidelity, visual design, user interfaces
Duration: 1 month

Logo refresh

The current logo lacks coherence and resonance. Our strategic endeavor entailed accentuating the 'L' in ALPFA, thereby articulating a profound connection to the identity and cultural tapestry of its esteemed membership base.

Updated Brand Identity

Crafting a dynamic and inviting brand identity involved a refreshing reinterpretation of fonts and shapes. We deftly balanced this with adherence to certain elements of the original color palette, as per the client's specifications.

Section Exploration

Our exploration delved into the intricate dynamics of website sections, contemplating their individual existence and fostering interconnectedness on each page.
