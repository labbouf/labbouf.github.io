Tutor Orial

2020

Identity + Digital Product Design
This eight-week initiative aimed to enhance Tutor Orial's scheduling system, offering a more seamless experience for users. Tutor Orial, catering to students with its engaging and skilled tutors, sought to streamline its scheduling process to boost completion rates and reduce confusion, evidenced by complaints or negative feedback.

+visual design, research, user experience, user testing, high-fidelity prototyping
Duration: 8 weeks

Timeline

The project unfolded in three stages: initial problem discovery through usability heuristic evaluation, followed by ideation with diverse flow creation and testing. The second stage involved testing low-fidelity prototypes, synthesizing feedback, and iterating designs. The final stage was dedicated to developing a distinctive design system and a high-fidelity prototype.
