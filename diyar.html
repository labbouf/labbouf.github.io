Diyar

2023

Identity + Digital Product Design
In Arabic, "Diyar" (ديار) translates to "homes" or "homelands." It is often used to refer to countries, regions, or places where people live and to which they feel a deep connection. The term evokes a sense of belonging and cultural identity.
The Diyar mobile app is tailored for discerning travelers seeking luxury accommodations, featuring a sleek, modern design. Beyond mere bookings, it offers a curated selection of premium stays, sourced from esteemed travel authorities such as Conde Nast Traveler and Travel + Leisure, and includes potential collaborations with verified travel influencers. Enhancing user experience through personalization, the app suggests specific stays and locations based on users' preferences and past bookings, ensuring a bespoke travel planning journey.

+persona & journey mapping, user flows & wireframes (concepts and detail design), ﻿﻿prototyping & interaction design, high-fidelity, visual design, graphic design, user interfaces
