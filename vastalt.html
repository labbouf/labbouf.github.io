Vast Alternatives

2015

Artwork
This artwork can be interpreted as a profound exploration of women's empowerment in the Middle East, particularly focusing on the diverse paths available to women in a rapidly evolving societal context. It encapsulates the myriad choices that women in regions like Saudi Arabia have, to define and shape their identities and destinies. The piece subtly yet powerfully comments on the changing roles and rights of women in a traditionally conservative society.

By highlighting three distinct representations, it echoes the multiplicity of women's experiences and aspirations in the Middle East. Each representation in the artwork could symbolize a different aspect of womanhood or a different path that a woman can choose.

In essence, this piece is not just a representation but a celebration of the courage and resilience of women in the Middle East, their journey towards empowerment, and the increasingly visible role they play in reshaping their societies. It stands as a testament to the fact that despite the challenges, women are increasingly asserting their right to choose their paths and express their individuality.

+art direction, stencil making, laser cutting
